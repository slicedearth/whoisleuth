WHOIS & RDAP Lookup

Queries the official IANA RDAP bootstrap and regional registries (ARIN, RIPE, APNIC, LACNIC, AFRINIC, and TLD registries), plus live WHOIS over port 43 starting from whois.iana.org. Enter one domain, IPv4/IPv6 address, or ASN for full detail, or paste/upload a list of domains for a bulk run with CSV export.

Data sourced directly from IANA's RDAP bootstrap service and the referring registry's own RDAP/WHOIS servers. This tool runs a small local backend so real WHOIS (TCP/43) and cross-origin RDAP requests are possible — browsers cannot make either of those calls directly. Most gTLD registries redact registrant contact details for privacy, so registrant fields are often blank — that reflects the registry's own privacy redaction, not a lookup failure.
